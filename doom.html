An ill defined concept such as doom surely must make you tremble. Like doom itself was on your doorstep. Or worse, inside a piano.
Surely you can appreciate the vague inaptitude that the word doom has to make you feel profound and mind-ravaging emotions. I'm sure you can do that.

But before appreciating this very thought, please think about the children, et tous les autres grands sages de ce vaillant monde.

Rêve Folinoïde
